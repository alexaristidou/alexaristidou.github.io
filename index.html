Hello, I'm Alexandros.

PhD Student in Organizational Behavior at UT Austin

I am a doctoral researcher in Organizational Behavior at the McCombs School of Business, The University of Texas at Austin. My interests lie in investigating how individuals and teams stay motivated, maintain well-being, and thrive in a psychologically safe environment. In addition, I am increasingly curious about how they can excel by effectively collaborating with emerging technology.

My transition into academia was driven by a desire to research the organizational realities I have experienced. From military to global corporate teams, I have lived across five continents and worked with people from across the world. This cross-cultural journey exposed me to how deeply identity, norms, and institutional environments shape human potential. Today, my goal is to translate those experiences into rigorous research aimed at helping people and teams in organizations thrive.

If you would like to chat about research, projects, or simply get in touch, feel free to reach out. Would love to connect.
